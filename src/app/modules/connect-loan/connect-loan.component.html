Connect Loan

Connect with your student loan servicer and we'll securely sync all your loan data.

Summary

Connecting with your servicer will pull in the following:

  • School name
  • Loan category (subsidized, unsubsidized)
  • Loan status history
  • Interest rates for each of your loans
  • Outstanding loan balances

? FAQs

How do you connect with my servicer?

We use Plaid, a service that securely connects with loan servicer. All data is encrypted using bank-level encyryption.
